üìâ Risk Factors and Uncertainties

This investment plan is subject to several risks. The most significant is Market Volatility. The 60% allocation to equities, particularly with an inclusion of Mid Cap funds, makes the portfolio highly susceptible to market downturns. Over a short 3-year period, there is insufficient time to recover from a potential correction, which could lead to a substantial loss of capital. Secondly, there is a clear Strategy-Horizon Mismatch. Standard financial planning principles advise against high equity exposure for short-term, critical goals. The current plan prioritizes growth at the expense of capital preservation, a flawed strategy for this scenario. Furthermore, the plan is based on an Unvalidated Return Assumption of 11% annually. This is a projection, not a guarantee. Actual market returns can and will vary. Lastly, the plan is provisional due to a Data Retrieval Failure, meaning it could not be benchmarked against historical performance data, making its theoretical soundness unverified.
