Market Risk & Volatility: With 70% invested in equities, and a significant portion in mid and small-cap funds, the portfolio's value will experience significant fluctuations in the short to medium term. Additionally, the portfolio includes two funds from Mirae Asset AMC. While both are strong performers, this introduces a minor concentration risk at the AMC level, which should be monitored.
