🏢 Company Overview

Asian Paints Ltd. was founded in 1942 by Champaklal Choksey, Chimanlal Choksi, Suryakant Dani, and Arvind Vakil. The company started as a small paint manufacturing unit in Bombay and has grown into India's largest paint company [1]. By 1967, Asian Paints had established itself as the market leader in the Indian paint industry through innovation and consumer focus [2].

Business Model

Asian Paints operates on both B2C and B2B models, catering to individual homeowners, contractors, architects, and industrial clients. The company is vertically integrated, controlling the value chain from raw material procurement to retail. Asian Paints has a diversified product portfolio that includes decorative paints, industrial coatings, waterproofing solutions, home improvement, and adhesives [REF].

Revenue Streams

Asian Paints generates revenue through multiple streams:

  • Decorative Paints: The primary revenue driver, including interior and exterior wall paints, wood and metal finishes [REF].
  • Industrial Coatings: Supplying industrial paints and coatings to automotive, packaging, and infrastructure industries.
  • Waterproofing Solutions: Sealants, roof coatings, and cementitious solutions marketed to homeowners and construction companies.
  • Home Improvement and Decor: Through the Asian Paints Beautiful Homes service, offering modular kitchens, wardrobes, furniture, and lighting.
  • Adhesives and Sealants: Products like tile adhesives, wood glues, and bonding solutions.
