These funds form the stable core of the equity portfolio. This provides a reliable foundation for long-term wealth creation.

Mid Cap Funds

This category serves as the portfolio's growth catalyst. Mid-cap companies are typically in a higher growth phase and possess the potential to deliver superior, market-beating returns. The 12-year investment horizon provides an adequate buffer to absorb the higher volatility associated with mid-caps.

Debt Allocation Rationale (50%)

Corporate Bond Funds

These funds offer an effective balance of safety and yield. They invest in debt securities of reputable corporations, typically providing higher interest income than government bonds. This contributes positively to the portfolio's overall return while maintaining a risk profile significantly lower than equities.

Short Duration Funds

This category enhances portfolio stability and mitigates interest rate risk. By investing in debt instruments with shorter maturities, these funds are less sensitive to fluctuations in interest rates, making them a more stable component of the debt portfolio.
