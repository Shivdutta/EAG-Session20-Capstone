Investing in mutual funds via SIP involves inherent market risks, and it is crucial to understand them before proceeding. While this plan is designed to align with a 'low_moderate' risk profile, it does not eliminate risk entirely. Market Risk is the primary concern; the value of equity investments can fluctuate significantly due to economic changes, political events, and market sentiment. The 9-year horizon helps mitigate this but does not guarantee positive returns. Inflation Risk is another key factor; if the actual rate of education inflation exceeds the projected 5.5%, the final corpus may be insufficient to meet the goal. For the debt portion, Interest Rate Risk can affect the value of bond funds; if interest rates rise, the value of existing bonds may fall. Finally, as noted, this plan was formulated without specific fund performance data, representing an Information Risk. It is imperative to perform due diligence on specific funds before investing. Regular portfolio review and rebalancing are essential to stay on track and adapt to changing market conditions.
